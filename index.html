By harnessing the power of photography and AI, our inclusive and aspirational image resource will redefine who we see as leaders, changemakers and innovators.

VISION

All In Images is building a new visual landscape for the future — one where photography and AI converge to radically redefine perceptions of who holds power, shapes culture, and drives change in every sector globally. We challenge the stereotypes of the past and replace them with inclusive imagery that reflect the world not as it is, but as it can be.

WHO WE ARE

Deirdre Cijffers
Founder

Deirdre Cijffers is an accomplished online education specialist renowned for designing innovative digital learning experiences. Her special interest is in transformative, accessible education solutions for diverse, global student communities.

For decades representing these diverse communities in her work has been a challenge, one she is aiming to solve with All In Images.

Betsy Richards-Kemp
Photographer

I received my first camera as a child and that hobby grew into studying photography at university. More recently, I have taken those experiences into a career as a professional photographer. I have an interest in researching and observing subjects within social contexts, to bring meaning to my images.

I was keen to work with All In Images as the aim of promoting diversity within emerging technologies matches my approach to photography.

https://br424.myportfolio.com

alBasir Sheikh
Photographer

alBasir Sheikh is a Photographer and a Creative practitioner. He has a distinct skill set in photography and image-making, developed upon years of research and practice.

Documentary and Photojournalism are the most intriguing areas of his practice. Alongside conventional uses of photography, Basir also likes to explore the boundaries of his practice through his experimental work, which is one reason that made him interested in this project.

www.albasirsheikh.com

Hannah O'Brien
Non-Executive Director

Hannah is an entrepreneurial marketing and business development specialist with a proven track record in scaling early-stage tech companies. Experienced in driving multi-million-pound revenue through B2B sales, grants, and prize funds, Hannah brings valuable commercial insight and strategic oversight to the All In Images team.

Vicky Hayford
